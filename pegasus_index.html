Who we are

A direct bridge between Colombian farmers and specialty buyers.

Based in Cúcuta, Norte de Santander, Pegasus Global Trade was founded on a simple belief: the best coffee comes from genuine relationships with the people who grow it.

We visit farms across Colombia's finest coffee regions, understanding each producer's story, their land, and their process. We select only micro-lots with exceptional sensory profiles and full traceability — then export them directly to specialty roasters and cafés in the United States.

No middlemen. No compromises. Just great Colombian coffee reaching the people who value it most.

—
Genuine farmer relationships We negotiate directly and honestly, contributing to sustainable livelihoods in coffee-growing communities.
—
Full traceability Every lot comes with complete origin data: farm, region, process, elevation, and sensory profile.
—
Small lots, exceptional quality We specialize in micro and nano lots — up to 60 kg green — allowing for focused selection and consistent quality.

How we work

From farm to export,
with care at every step.

Sourcing

Direct farm visits

We travel to farms across Norte de Santander, Huila, and the Eje Cafetero. We build real relationships with producers, understand their processes, and evaluate lots in person — not through catalogs.

Selection

Quality evaluation

Each lot is evaluated for sensory profile, traceability, altitude, processing method, and exportability. We only move forward with coffees that meet specialty standards and have a clear, honest story to tell.

Export

Documentation & delivery

We handle all export logistics — phytosanitary certificates, customs documentation, and shipping coordination. Small lots, up to 60 kg green, delivered directly to your roastery in the US.
