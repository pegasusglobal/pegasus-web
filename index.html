Services

What we offer.

Direct sourcing

We source green specialty coffee directly from farms across Colombia, with full traceability from farm to export.

Export & logistics

How we work

From farm to export,
with care at every step.

Farm visit — photo placeholder
01

Direct farm visits

We travel to farms across Norte de Santander, Huila, and the Eje Cafetero — building real relationships with producers and evaluating lots in person.

02

Quality evaluation

Each lot is cupped and evaluated for sensory profile, traceability, altitude, and exportability. Only coffees meeting specialty standards move forward.

03

Documentation & delivery

We handle all export logistics — phytosanitary certificates, customs, and shipping. Small lots delivered directly to your roastery in the US.
